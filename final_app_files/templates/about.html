About Us

The premise of our project: We want to determine if publicly available information about corporations could be collected, instantiated and manipulated by machine learning models to predict if a stock would be a good buy and hold for one year, then sell for a profit. Our final machine learning model will show based on the data (annual 10K data reported by companies to the securities exchange commission) if the price is predicted increase or decrease during that time frame. Using data from five years and from many companies financials, we created a training dataset, tuned our model, and made our predictions. Our data has been presented on our front-end by our web development team.
